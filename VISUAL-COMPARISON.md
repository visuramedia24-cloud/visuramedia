## 🧠 PSYCHOLOGICAL PRINCIPLES USED

### ✅ **1. Contrast Effect**
- Show good scores FIRST
- Then reveal the ONE bad score
- Makes the problem stand out more

### ✅ **2. Authority Positioning**
- "Most people only look at the average"
- "But WE know Performance matters"
- Positions you as expert who sees what others miss

### ✅ **3. Loss Aversion**
- "40% of visitors bounce"
- "€1,500/month lost"
- People fear loss more than they desire gain

### ✅ **4. Social Proof**
- "Google bewertet nur Performance"
- "Fortune 500 companies use this tool"
- If Google cares, it must be important

### ✅ **5. Scarcity**
- "7 Tage gültig"
- "Nur 3 Plätze"
- Creates urgency to act now

### ✅ **6. Specificity**
- Not "your site is slow"
- But "LCP: 4.9s → 53% bounce"
- Specific numbers = credibility
